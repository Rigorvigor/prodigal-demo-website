Edge AI

With the increase in the number of customers using edge devices like mobile, IoT devices, smart speakers, robots etc to access different services, businesses increasingly realize the need to bring essential computation onto the device to serve better. Edge AI utilizes advanced analytical methods like machine learning and AI to perform computing locally thereby enabling an unbeatable combination of better latency, energy efficiency, real-time analytics, scalability, information security and privacy.

The automatic decision making becomes feasible due to the reduced latency and scalability of analytics, which optimizes the overhead cost. Moreover, Edge AI is an innovative technology that promises positive results with the growth in cloud computing and big data.

Computer Vision

Have you ever imagined providing your computer with an eye, just like humans, which can be used to derive useful information from the surroundings and take quicker decisions? AI enables systems to think and make decisions, but Computer vision enables them to see, observe, understand and tune in with the surroundings to mimic decisions just like humans. Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence (AI) that enables computers and systems to derive meaningful information from digital images, videos and other visual inputs, and take actions or make recommendations based on that information.

Transportation

The increasing demands of the transportation sector have propelled technological development in this industry, with computer vision at its centre. Computer vision is used to detect and classify objects (e.g., road signs or traffic lights), create 3D maps or motion estimation, and play a key role in making autonomous vehicles a reality. Pedestrian detection and tracking have become a vital computer vision research area. Computer vision is proven cost-effective for Parking Guidance and Information (PGI) systems too.

Healthcare

Without the right technology in place, doctors were forced to spend hours manually analyzing patient data and doing administrative work. With the introduction of Computer Vision in healthcare several tasks like X-ray analysis, CT & MRI, cancer detection, blood loss management, digital pathology, movement analysis etc has become more efficient and time-saving.

Manufacturing

The manufacturing industry has already adopted CV in day to day tasks like defect detection, texts and barcode reading, product assembly etc.

Object Recognition

Object recognition is a general term to describe a collection of related computer vision tasks that involve identifying objects in digital photographs. Image classification involves predicting the class of one object in an image. Object localization refers to identifying the location of one or more objects in an image and drawing an abounding box around their extent. Object detection combines these two tasks and localizes and classifies one or more objects in an image. This CV technique is used in manufacturing, construction, healthcare, transportation, intelligent survelliance and video analytics.

Drone Intelligence

Drones are unmanned, aerial devices used for a variety of purposes. Drones often incorporate artificial intelligence, for automation. The incorporation of AI enables drone vendors to use data from sensors attached to the drone to collect and implement visual and environmental data, that enables autonomous or assisted flight, making operation easier and increasing accessibility. The computer vision technology enables drones to detect objects while flying and allows the analysis and recording of information on the ground.

To implement AI in drones, we first train the machine learning algorithms to object detection and classification, by feeding specially marked images into the algorithm. These images teach the AI which traits classes of objects have and how to differentiate one type of object from another. More advanced AI enables real-time detection, improving analysis with use.

Cloud Analytics

Cloud analytics describes the application of analytic algorithms in the cloud against data in a private or public cloud to deliver a result of interest. An amalgamation of AI into identifying patterns in data and extracting new insights can gain a competitive advantage, advance scientific discovery, or improve life in all sorts of ways. AI based analytics engines, dashboards and results help cloud vendors as well as end customers to make better decisions.

Cloud cost can also be optimized by the insights derived from AI recommendation engines, which requires negligible technical literacy for the vendors.

Fintech AI

AI is a critical part of the fintech space in terms of collecting data, analysing information, safeguarding and facilitating transactions and marking financial decisions. In the past few years, stock trading decisions have been highly automated with tools for opportunity detection, that can identify different profitable trading opportunities like arbitrage opportunities etc in the market, and generate trade signals accordingly.

These types of AI system comes with an alert mechanism that notifies the trader for the execution of trade for maximum benefit. Due to the involvement of risk factors and several other factors that affect the stock market, AI can be a great technology to build systems for investment strategy simulation. These systems are capable to stimulate a trading decision based on real-time data with different stock market strategies that help in automated portfolio allocation.

Blockchain & Crypto

Pairing blockchain and AI enhances data security, increases transparent management of data by creating a trustworthy data economy. AI models embedded in smart contracts executed on a blockchain can recommend expired products to recall, execute transactions, such as re-orders, payments, or stock purchases based on set thresholds and events and select the most sustainable method.

The AI-based crypto risk management model that considers the DEFI characteristics and risk as two important features can help the clients in managing the risk factor while investing in cryptocurrencies. AI can be used to design several crypto investment strategy simulation tools that can provide a simulation of trades on different strategies involved with different protocols to the clients considering the risk and profit.
